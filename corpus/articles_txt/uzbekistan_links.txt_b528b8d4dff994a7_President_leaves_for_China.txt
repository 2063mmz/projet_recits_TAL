President leaves for China
24.04.2019
President of the Republic of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev has left today, 24 April, for Beijing on a working visit at the invitation of President of the People’s Republic of China Xi Jinping.
The head of our state is set to take part in the second International Forum “One Belt, One Road”. Shavkat Mirziyoyev will meet with Chinese President Xi Jinping and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People’s Congress Li Zhanshu. A number of bilateral talks are also planned with heads of delegations of foreign countries and international organizations attending the forum.
