H.E. Hadera Abera Holds Discussions with Agility CEO on Expanding Investment Cooperation
H.E. Hadera Abera, State Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia, held fruitful discussions with Ms. Msadi Anwar Al-Saleh, Chairperson and Chief Executive Officer of Agility, on October 15, 2025, focusing on expanding investment cooperation in key economic sectors.
The discussion centered on shared interest in advancing strategic partnerships across the development of modern logistics hubs, sector-specific industrial parks, aviation ground and cargo handling services, digital customs systems, e-commerce facilitation, as well as fuel transport and storage infrastructure.
