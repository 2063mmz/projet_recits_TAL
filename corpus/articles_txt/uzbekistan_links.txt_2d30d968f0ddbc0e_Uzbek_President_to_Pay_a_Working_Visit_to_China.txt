Uzbek President to Pay a Working Visit to China
16.10.2023
At the invitation of President of the People's Republic of China Xi Jinping, President of the Republic of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev will pay a working visit to Beijing on October 17-18 to participate in the events of the Third International Forum "One Belt, One Road".
The summit’s agenda includes the further development of trade and economic cooperation, strengthening transport and communication connectivity, promoting green development programs, digitalization and other areas. The main results of the multilateral partnership over the past ten years will be summed up, and new interaction tasks for the future will be identified.
The President of Uzbekistan is expected to deliver a keynote speech at the forum’s plenary session and outline his vision of current international cooperation issues within the framework of the Belt and Road Initiative.
The program of stay also includes talks with the President of the People’s Republic of China Xi Jinping and Chairman of the Chinese People’s Political Consultative Conference Wang Huning, and other bilateral meetings.
