Vladimir Putin arrived in China
Vladimir Putin arrived in Beijing for a working visit, where he will attend the second Belt and Road International Forum.
April 25, 2019
15:50
Beijing
Arrival in Beijing.
The Russian President will also hold
talks with President of the People’s Republic of China Xi Jinping to discuss the further development of Russian-Chinese comprehensive partnership and strategic
cooperation, as well as current regional and international affairs.
In addition to this, a number of bilateral meetings
are planned between the President of Russia and the leaders of the countries
taking part in the forum.
