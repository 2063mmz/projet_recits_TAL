Official welcoming ceremony for participants of the Third Belt and Road Forum
October 17, 2023
14:05
Beijing
Vladimir Putin took part in the official welcoming
ceremony held by President of the People’s Republic of China Xi Jinping and his
spouse Peng Liyuan for the heads of delegations taking part in the Third Belt
and Road Forum for International Cooperation.
The ceremony and posing for photographs, followed by a gala reception, took
place in the Great Hall of the People in Beijing.
* * *
The President of Russia arrived in China at the invitation of President Xi Jinping. He held several bilateral meetings on the sidelines of the international forum.
