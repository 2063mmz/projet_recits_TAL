Mikhail Mishustin’s telephone conversation with Prime Minister of Belarus Alexander Turchin
22 December 2025
19:40
Prime Minister Mikhail Mishustin had
a telephone conversation with Prime Minister of the Republic of Belarus Alexander
Turchin.
The prime ministers discussed current
issues of the bilateral trade, economic, scientific and technical cooperation, as well as the progress in joint projects in energy, industry, agriculture,
transport infrastructure, and others.
Mikhail Mishustin and Alexander Turchin
highlighted the importance of promoting the integration cooperation within the Union State of Russia and Belarus and within the Eurasian Economic Union.
