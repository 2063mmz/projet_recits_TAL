Visit to China. Belt and Road Forum for International Cooperation
October 17 − 18, 2023
Visit abroad, 12 events
In Beijing, the Russian President took part in the 3
Belt and Road Forum for International Cooperation. He spoke at the opening ceremony, and later met with President of the People's Republic of China Xi
Jinping.
Vladimir Putin had a series of bilateral meetings on the sidelines of the forum. He met with President of Vietnam Vo Van Thuong, Prime Minister of Hungary Viktor Orban, Prime Minister
of Thailand Srettha Thavisin, President of Mongolia Ukhnaagiin Khurelsukh,
President of Laos Thongloun Sisoulith, and Caretaker Prime Minister of Pakistan
Anwaar-ul-Haq Kakar.
Concluding his visit to China, Vladimir Putin answered the Russian media’s questions.
