State Minister H.E. Ambassador Hadera Abera Admasu co-chaired the inaugural Ethio-Belgium Joint Political Consultations alongside H.E. Theodora Gentzis, Secretary-General of the Belgian Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
The two sides reaffirmed their commitment to deepening the longstanding and enduring bilateral relationship and explored new avenues for economic cooperation, multilateral engagement, and partnership across key sectors.
The consultations highlighted both nations’ shared interest in advancing sustainable development, trade, and diplomatic collaboration, reflecting the robust foundation of Ethiopia–Belgium relations.
