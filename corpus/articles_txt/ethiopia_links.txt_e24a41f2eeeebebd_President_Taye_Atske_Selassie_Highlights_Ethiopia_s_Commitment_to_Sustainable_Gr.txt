President Taye Atske Selassie Highlights Ethiopia’s Commitment to Sustainable Growth at Second Global Gateway Forum
H.E. President Taye Atske Selassie addressed the opening of the Second Global Gateway Forum in Brussels, reaffirming Ethiopia’s readiness to actively engage in the Global Gateway Initiative.
The President emphasized that Ethiopia’s ambitions in renewable energy development form the foundation of its prosperity, supported by a progressive investment ecosystem and a strategic approach to connectivity that enhances international cooperation.
He underlined Ethiopia’s commitment to advancing sustainable and inclusive growth through strengthened global partnerships under the Global Gateway framework, aimed at promoting green transformation, resilient infrastructure, and shared prosperity across Africa.
