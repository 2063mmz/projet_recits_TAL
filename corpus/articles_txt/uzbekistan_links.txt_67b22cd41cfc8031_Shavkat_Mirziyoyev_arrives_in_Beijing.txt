Shavkat Mirziyoyev arrives in Beijing
24.04.2019
President of the Republic of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev has arrived in Beijing on a working visit to the People’s Republic of China.
At the Shoudu (Beijing Capital) International Airport, the head of our state was met by Chinese officials.
The first “One Belt, One Road” International Forum, where President Shavkat Mirziyoyev also was present, took place in 2017. This time around, the summit is expected to host more than 40 heads of state and government, leaders of international and regional organizations.
The forum agenda includes discussion of issues related to the enhancement of economic cooperation, enlargement of interaction in search of new sources for long-term growth, promotion of green economy and innovation, and realization of priority infrastructure projects in the region.
The main events of the visit are to start tomorrow, April 25.
President Shavkat Mirziyoyev is expected to hold meetings with PRC President Xi Jinping, leaders of a number of countries and international financial institutions, as well as major Chinese companies.
