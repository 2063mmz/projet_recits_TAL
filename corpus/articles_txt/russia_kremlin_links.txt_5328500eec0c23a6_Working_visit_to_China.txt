Working visit to China
Vladimir Putin took part in the events of the
Second Belt
and
Road
International
Forum.
After that the Russian
President held talks with President of the People’s Republic of China Xi
Jinping. Also, the President of Russia received anhonorary doctorate from
Tsinghua
University
, one of China’s leading universities.
On the following day Vladimir Putin
took part in the first session of the round table discussion at the International
Belt
and
Road
Forum.
After the round table the President answered journalists’
questions.
As part of his working visit to China the Russian President also had a number of bilateral meetings with heads of state who attended the forum.

Beijing
