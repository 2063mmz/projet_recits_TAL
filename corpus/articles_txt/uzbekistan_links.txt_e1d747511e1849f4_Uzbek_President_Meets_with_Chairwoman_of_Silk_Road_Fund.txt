Uzbek President Meets with Chairwoman of Silk Road Fund
25.01.2024
As part of the program of stay in Shenzhen, the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev held a meeting with Zhu Jun, the Chairwoman of Silk Road Fund.
The Fund was established in 2014 on the initiative of the President of the People’s Republic of China Xi Jinping to provide financial support for trade, economic and investment cooperation within the project «One Belt, One Road».
Joint plans for the implementation of promising projects by actively exploiting the investment platform established with the Fund to provide financial support for industrial cooperation and infrastructure development were considered.
