Ethiopia Advocates for a Sudanese-Led Peace Process and Enhanced International Support at the London Conference
At the London Sudan Conference, Ethiopia underscored the critical need for a Sudanese-led peace process, a sustained ceasefire to safeguard civilians, and a robust increase in international support for humanitarian assistance and rfugee aid.
Ethiopia remains steadfast in its leadership, driving efforts to facilitate coordinated relief and foster lasting peace in Sudan amidst the ongoing crisis.
